Our Courses

At Dr. Shyama Prasad Mukherjee University, we offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses designed to prepare students for the challenges of the modern world. Explore our diverse offerings below:

Bachelor of Science in Computer Applications

Learn the foundations of programming, web development, and software engineering to kickstart your career in technology.

  • Duration: 3 Years
  • Key Subjects: Data Structures, Web Development, AI & ML, Cybersecurity
  • Career Prospects: Software Developer, Data Analyst, IT Consultant

Bachelor of Arts in English Literature

Dive into the world of classic and contemporary literature while honing your analytical and communication skills.

  • Duration: 3 Years
  • Key Subjects: Shakespearean Studies, Literary Criticism, World Literature
  • Career Prospects: Content Writer, Editor, Journalist, Educator

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

Develop leadership and managerial skills to thrive in the corporate world.

  • Duration: 3 Years
  • Key Subjects: Marketing, Finance, HR Management, Business Strategy
  • Career Prospects: Business Analyst, Marketing Executive, Entrepreneur

Master of Science in Environmental Science

Address pressing environmental challenges with advanced knowledge in sustainable development and ecology.

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Key Subjects: Environmental Chemistry, Climate Change, Biodiversity
  • Career Prospects: Environmental Consultant, Conservation Scientist

Diploma in Graphic Design

Master the art of design and visual storytelling with industry-standard tools.

  • Duration: 1 Year
  • Key Subjects: Photoshop, Illustrator, UX/UI Design
  • Career Prospects: Graphic Designer, UI/UX Designer, Creative Director
